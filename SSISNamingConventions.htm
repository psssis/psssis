In 2006 Jamie Thomson came up with naming conventions for SSIS tasks and data flow components.
These naming conventions make your packages and logs more readable. Five versions and a decade
later a couple of tasks and components where deprecated, but there where are also over 40 new tasks
and components introduced by Microsoft. This page show a new complete list with prefix suggestions
for all new tasks and components (marked with *).
